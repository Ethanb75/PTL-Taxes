About PTL

At PTL Management & Tax Services, we believe that all of our clients are entitled to the highest level of service. Our tax professionals undergo the most rigorous training program, so they know how to maximize each and every one of the returns they prepare. In other words, we ensure that you will benefit from every deduction and credit available to you. Our associate training and system of safeguards are carefully designed to ensure the accuracy of your return. We offer Audit Guard protection, which provides you with cash reimbursements of up to $3,000 towards your IRS bill in qualified cases (limitations apply). We also offer many other services.
